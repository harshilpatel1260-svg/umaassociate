Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions related to accounting, taxation and financial advisory services

Who should file income tax return?

Any individual, professional or business whose income exceeds the basic exemption limit or meets other specified conditions under the Income Tax Act should file ITR.

What is GST registration and who needs it?

GST registration is mandatory for businesses whose turnover exceeds prescribed limits or those involved in inter-state supply, e-commerce or notified categories.
