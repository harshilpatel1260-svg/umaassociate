Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about accounting, taxation, compliance and advisory services.

When should I file my Income Tax Return?
Generally, individuals must file their Income Tax Return by 31st July of the assessment year unless extended by the government. Businesses and audited cases have different due dates.
Is GST registration mandatory for small businesses?
GST registration is mandatory if turnover exceeds prescribed limits or if the business engages in inter-state supply, e-commerce or certain notified activities.
Do you handle tax notices and assessments?
Yes, we provide end-to-end support for tax notices, replies, scrutiny assessments, appeals and departmental proceedings.
Can you assist startups with funding compliance?
Absolutely. We assist startups with structuring, compliance, ESOP advisory, due diligence, pitch deck financials and investor readiness.
Do you offer online consultation services?
Yes, we provide online consultations via phone, WhatsApp and video calls for clients across India and overseas.
