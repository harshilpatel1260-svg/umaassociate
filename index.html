Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about accounting, GST and tax compliance

When should I file my Income Tax Return?
Generally, individuals must file their Income Tax Return by 31st July of the assessment year, unless extended by the government through notification.
Is GST registration mandatory for small businesses?
GST registration is mandatory if your aggregate turnover exceeds the prescribed threshold or if you engage in inter-state supply or e-commerce operations.
Do you handle income tax notices and assessments?
Yes, we provide end-to-end support for income tax notices, scrutiny assessments, rectifications and departmental proceedings.
Can you manage complete accounting and compliance for startups?
Absolutely. We provide startup registration, accounting system setup, GST onboarding, monthly compliance, tax planning and investor reporting support.
Do you offer online consultation services?
Yes, we offer online consultations through phone, WhatsApp and video calls across India.
