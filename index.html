Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions related to accounting, taxation, GST compliance and financial advisory services.

Who should file income tax return?

Any individual, professional or business whose income exceeds the basic exemption limit or meets other specified conditions under the Income Tax Act should file an income tax return every year.

What is GST registration and who needs it?

GST registration is mandatory for businesses whose turnover exceeds prescribed limits or those involved in inter-state supply, e-commerce or notified categories under GST law.

What are the benefits of professional accounting services?

Professional accounting ensures accurate financial records, better cash flow control, improved compliance, informed business decisions and reduced risk of penalties.

Can you help with income tax and GST notices?

Yes. We provide complete support for income tax and GST notices, replies, assessments, appeals and ongoing compliance management.
