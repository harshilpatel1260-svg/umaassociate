Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions related to income tax, GST, accounting, and business compliance.

Who needs to file Income Tax Return in India?
Any individual or entity whose income exceeds the basic exemption limit, or who wants to claim refunds, carry forward losses, or comply with statutory requirements, should file an Income Tax Return.
Is GST registration mandatory for small businesses?
GST registration is mandatory if your aggregate turnover exceeds the threshold limit prescribed by the government or if you are involved in interstate supply or e-commerce.
What documents are required for ITR filing?
PAN, Aadhaar, Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs, and other income-related documents are typically required for filing ITR.
