Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, processes and pricing.

When should I file my Income Tax Return? +

Generally, Income Tax Returns should be filed on or before 31st July of the assessment year for individuals who are not required to get their accounts audited. Due dates may vary for businesses and audited cases.

Who needs GST registration? +

Businesses with turnover exceeding the prescribed threshold limit or engaged in inter-state supply, e-commerce or certain notified categories must obtain GST registration.

What documents are required for ITR filing? +

Common documents include PAN, Aadhaar, Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs, TDS certificates, capital gains statements and details of other income sources.

Do you provide notice handling and assessment support? +

Yes, we provide professional representation and support for Income Tax, GST and other statutory notices and assessments.

Can you handle accounting and compliance for startups? +

Absolutely. We specialize in providing end-to-end accounting, taxation and compliance solutions for startups and SMEs.
